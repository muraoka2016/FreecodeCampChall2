Kurt Cobain

Legendary frontman and leader of Nirvana

Kurt Cobain, captured in a serene profile shot, is deeply immersed in playing his acoustic guitar.

Here is a timeline of important events in Kurt Cobain's life:

  • 20, 1967 - Kurt Donald Cobain was born in Aberdeen, Washington, United States.
  • 1976 - First Contact with Music Kurt received his first guitar at the age of 9, a gift from an uncle, sparking his interest in music.
  • 1985 - Formation of the Band Fecal Matter Cobain formed his first band, Fecal Matter, with his school friends, which would later lead to the formation of Nirvana.
  • 1987 - Nirvana is Founded Kurt Cobain, Krist Novoselic, and Aaron Burckhard officially formed the band Nirvana in Aberdeen, Washington.
  • June 15, 1989 - Release of the First Album, Bleach Nirvana released their debut album, Bleach, which began to gain attention in the Seattle grunge scene.
  • September 24, 1991 - Release of Nevermind Nirvana’s second album, Nevermind, was released, with the single "Smells Like Teen Spirit" propelling the band to global success.
  • February 24, 1992 - Marriage to Courtney Love Kurt Cobain married singer Courtney Love in a ceremony in Hawaii.
  • August 18, 1992 - Birth of Frances Bean Cobain Kurt and Courtney had their only child, Frances Bean Cobain.
  • March 1, 1994 - Nirvana’s Last Concert Nirvana performed their last concert in Munich, Germany, before Kurt Cobain withdrew from the European tour due to health issues.
  • April 5, 1994 - Kurt Cobain’s Death Kurt Cobain passed away at the age of 27 in his Seattle home, a tragic event that shocked the music world.
